Our group is friendly, relaxed, and always open to new members. Whether you love diving deep into characters and themes or simply enjoy discovering new stories with others, you’ll find a place here. Come for the books, stay for the community, and be part of engaging conversations that connect us through the joy of reading.

History

Founded in 1999 in the cozy living room of one of its first members, the group began as a small circle of readers who loved sharing books and lively conversation. Over time, the club grew in spirit and purpose, eventually moving to a library setting to create a more welcoming, community-focused club. While it still enjoys a dedicated core membership, the group continues to look forward—inviting new readers to join, share their perspectives, and help shape the next chapter of its history.

Come and be part of our next chapter!
