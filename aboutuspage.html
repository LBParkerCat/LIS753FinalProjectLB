Our group is friendly, relaxed, and always open to new members. Whether you love diving deep into characters and themes or simply enjoy discovering new stories with others, you’ll find a place here. Come for the books, stay for the community, and be part of engaging conversations that connect us through the joy of reading.

History

Our Main sale offers adult books, CDs, DVDs, records, and magazines. Hardcover books start at $1.00 and paperback books start at $0.50. Main sale hours are 11AM-4PM SAT & 1PM-4PM SUN. The Main room faces East Charleston Rd: find it on our Sale room map. Check out our Bargain sale for even better deals!

If you want first crack on SAT, we use a ticketing system to ensure smooth traffic flow. We hand out up to 2 numbered tickets per person at the Main sale door, starting at 8AM.

Your tickets reserve you places in line when the sale starts at 11AM. You are free to get coffee or check out our Children's and Bargain sales in the meantime. Just get back to the Main room a few minutes before 11AM: you'll forfeit your spot(s) if you're not present. No ticket, no problem! Just join the line and we'll let you in as soon as we can.

We love our early shoppers, but during the first hour of the SAT sale there is a 12 book limit on purchases. You may rejoin the line to enter again. The 12 book limit remains in effect until there is no line

Why the numbers and line and puchase limit? We care about your health and safety, and the size of our Main room limits occupancy to 160 people. Our ticketing system allows early shopping. Our line system and purchase limit allow walk-up customers access. Everybody wins, and so does the library!
