Join our monthly Library Book Club, a welcoming community of readers who enjoy thoughtful conversation and the shared experience of great books. We meet once a month to explore literary fiction—from contemporary award-winners to timeless classics—with an occasional nonfiction title that sparks curiosity and discussion.

Our group is friendly, relaxed, and always open to new members. Whether you love diving deep into characters and themes or simply enjoy discovering new stories with others, you’ll find a place here. Come for the books, stay for the community, and be part of engaging conversations that connect us through the joy of reading.

What to expect

You may have some questions about our club. Here's how we roll:

  • Monthly meetings at the library — easygoing, welcoming, and inclusive
  • Flexibility — attend every month or once a year
  • Books reserved for you ahead of time — read at your own pace
  • You don’t have to be an library member/cardholder to participate
  • You don’t have to finish the book to join the discussion
  • Engaging discussions about characters, themes, and perspectives
  • A friendly group where all voices are valued
  • Light refreshments — several members enjoy baking and coffee/tea provided

Why join us

There are so many benefits to joining a book club (especially the Whippoorwills) that we can't list them all, but here are a few:

  • A book group introduces you to authors, genres, and stories you might never pick up on your own
  • Discussion brings a book to life
  • Books let you step into someone else’s shoes
  • Exploring why characters make certain choices helps sharpen your analytical and reflective skills
  • Book groups create a welcoming space to connect with others
  • Knowing you’ll discuss a book gives you a gentle nudge to read regularly
  • Taking time each month to read and chat with others is restorative
  • Books open doors to history, culture, philosophy, science, and more

What's not to like? Whether you’re a lifelong reader or just getting started, we’d love to have you.
